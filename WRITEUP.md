# Brief Write-Up

## Design Rationale

The original direction of the project started as an interactive website experience. After reviewing the assignment expectations and Digideck references more carefully, the project was redesigned into a guided deck-style presentation experience.

The final version focuses on controlled storytelling rather than free website browsing. Each section functions as a dedicated presentation slide with intentional pacing, slide progression, and business-focused interaction.

The experience was designed to simulate how a premium sales representative could present Dubai Mall opportunities to potential tenants, sponsors, and event partners during a live pitch session.

---

## Presentation Structure

The deck is organized into six primary slides:

1. Arrival
2. Scale
3. Destination Worlds
4. Events & Brand Platform
5. Opportunity Paths
6. Final CTA

This structure creates a clear narrative flow from introduction, to scale, to business opportunities, and finally conversion.

---

## Visual Direction

The visual design was inspired by:

- Digideck presentation systems
- Apple keynote storytelling
- Luxury commercial presentations
- Premium destination branding

The interface uses dark cinematic layouts, large typography, gold accent colors, modular content blocks, and controlled transitions to create a more immersive deck experience.

---

## Interaction Philosophy

Instead of long scrolling pages, the experience uses:

- Slide-based navigation
- Next / Previous presentation controls
- Interactive business modules
- Modal detail exploration
- Focused presentation framing

This helps maintain storytelling structure while still allowing selective exploration.

---

## AI Usage

AI tools were used throughout the iterative design process to help with:

- Structuring the presentation flow
- UX ideation
- Content refinement
- Interaction suggestions
- Styling iterations
- Documentation assistance

The final implementation, debugging, restructuring, and customization were manually completed and refined.

---

## Future Improvements

With more time, I would:

- Add custom motion transitions between slides
- Integrate official Dubai Mall media assets
- Expand business paths into multi-slide sub-decks
- Add presentation analytics and tracking
- Improve responsive presentation behavior on mobile devices
