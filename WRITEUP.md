# Brief Write-Up

## Design Rationale

I designed this project as an interactive sales deck rather than a traditional website. The goal was to create a premium, business-focused experience that could be used by a sales team to present Dubai Mall to potential tenants, sponsors, and event partners.

The visual direction is inspired by luxury brand websites, Apple-style product storytelling, and Digideck-style interactive presentations. I used a dark premium interface, gold accents, large typography, cinematic spacing, and modular navigation to create a high-end destination feel.

## Product Strategy

The experience is structured around the main business goals of the assignment: retail leasing, sponsorship opportunities, and event booking. Instead of forcing the user through a linear slide deck, the application allows non-linear exploration through side navigation and clickable business modules.

The “Worlds” section presents Dubai Mall as more than a shopping center by highlighting retail, luxury, dining, and attractions. The “Choose Your Opportunity” section turns the experience into a business tool by giving prospects clear paths based on their goals.

## AI Usage

I used AI tools to accelerate content structuring, UX planning, copywriting, and design iteration. AI helped generate sales-oriented messaging, organize the deck into business-focused sections, and refine the interaction model. I also used AI support to improve the README, project structure, and submission preparation.

## Future Improvements

With more time, I would replace placeholder imagery with official Dubai Mall media assets, add stronger motion design, include real venue data, expand each opportunity path into its own detailed module, and optimize the experience further for mobile devices.
